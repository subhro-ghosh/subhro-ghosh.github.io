About me

I am a post doc in the Dept of ORFE, Princeton University. I am broadly interested in probability theory and its applications. In particular, I have been working on determinantal point process, rigidity phenomena in point sets, random polynomials and random analytic functions and concentration of measure. My mentor at ORFE is Prof. Ramon van Handel. Before coming to Princeton, I obtained my PhD in Mathematics at the University of California, Berkeley, in May 2013 under the supervision of Prof. Yuval Peres. Before that, I did my Bachelor in Statistics and Master in Mathematics degrees at the Indian Statistical Institute.
